Academic Programs

Special Needs Curriculum

Our comprehensive special needs curriculum is individually tailored to address the unique learning requirements of each student. We employ differentiated instruction methods, adaptive teaching strategies, and personalized learning plans that accommodate various disabilities including physical, cognitive, sensory, and developmental challenges. Our curriculum emphasizes life skills, functional academics, and social-emotional development, ensuring every student reaches their full potential in a supportive and nurturing environment.

Mainstream Integration

Our mainstream integration program creates truly inclusive classrooms where students with special needs learn alongside their peers in regular academic settings. We provide co-teaching models, peer support systems, and universal design for learning principles that benefit all students. This approach fosters mutual understanding, respect, and social integration while maintaining high academic standards. Students develop empathy, leadership skills, and collaborative abilities, creating a diverse and enriched learning community for everyone.

Rehabilitation Center

Our comprehensive vision rehabilitation program provides specialized support for students who have lost sight or have visual impairments. We offer training in Braille literacy, orientation and mobility skills, daily living activities, and assistive technology for the visually impaired. Our certified vision specialists work one-on-one and in small groups to help students develop independence, confidence, and academic success. The program includes adaptive techniques for reading, writing, navigation, and accessing digital content, ensuring students can fully participate in all aspects of school life and beyond.

Ditigal Learning

Our computer learning program equips all students with essential digital literacy skills and modern technology competencies. We provide comprehensive training in basic computer operations, word processing, internet research, presentation software, and coding fundamentals. For students with special needs, we integrate assistive technologies including screen readers, voice recognition software, adaptive keyboards, and specialized input devices. Our state-of-the-art computer labs are fully accessible, ensuring every student can develop the technological skills necessary for academic success and future career opportunities in our digital world.
