About Our School

Discover what makes Kibos Special Senior School unique

Our Motto

Inspired for excellence

Our motto reflects our unwavering commitment to inspiring every student through quality education, regardless of their abilities or challenges. We believe that education has the power to transform lives and inspire greatness in every learner.

Our Mission

To provide high quality Secondary school education in partnership with stakeholders in order to empower students in all inclusive system to be self-reliant, disciplined, responsible and competitive for post-secondary academic pursuits and job opportunities.

Our Vision

A secondary school of excellence providing, high quality and affordable education for the visually impaired students within a multi-religious setting

Our Core Values

These fundamental principles guide everything we do and shape the culture of our school community.

Professionalism

We maintain the highest standards of professional conduct, demonstrating competence, dedication, and commitment to excellence in all our endeavors.

Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, and ethical principles in all our interactions, building trust within our school community.

Fear of God

We uphold spiritual values and moral principles, guiding our actions with reverence, humility, and a commitment to doing what is right.

Respect for self and others

We foster a culture of mutual respect, valuing ourselves and others, recognizing the dignity and worth of every individual in our community.

Time Consciousness

We value punctuality and efficient use of time, recognizing that time management is essential for personal growth and academic success.

Teamwork

We work together collaboratively, combining our strengths and supporting one another to achieve common goals and create a harmonious learning environment.
